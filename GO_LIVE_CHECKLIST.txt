═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  HOW IT WORKS (After Going Live)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CUSTOMER SIDE:
1. Sees your QR code (on car, flyer, etc.)
2. Opens phone camera
3. Points at QR code
4. Phone shows: "Open yourapp.onrender.com"
5. Taps notification
6. Form opens on their phone
7. Fills out ride details (30 seconds)
8. Submits
9. Gets confirmation message

YOUR SIDE:
1. Customer submits request
2. Data saved to your database
3. Open admin on any device:
   https://yourapp.onrender.com/admin
4. See new request
5. Click "Send Quote"
6. System calculates price
7. Confirm and send SMS to customer
8. Manage ride status

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  COMMON QUESTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: How much does it cost to run?
A: FREE with Render (with sleep), or $7/month always-on
   + Optional: Twilio SMS (~$7.50/100 messages)
   + Optional: Domain ($12/year)

Q: Can I use my phone to manage rides?
A: YES! Admin dashboard is fully mobile-optimized

Q: What if app is slow to load?
A: Free tier sleeps after 15 min. First load takes 30 sec.
   Upgrade to $7/month for instant loading.

Q: How do I update the app later?
A: Push changes to GitHub, Render auto-deploys

Q: Can I change admin password?
A: YES! Edit config.js and redeploy

Q: What if customer doesn't have data?
A: QR code works on WiFi too (hotels, cafes, etc.)

Q: Can I track how many scans?
A: Not built-in, but can add analytics later

Q: What happens to data if app restarts?
A: SQLite database persists, all data safe
